About Us

SWASTH

SWASTH is an innovative web-based tool designed to enhance personal health management.

It allows users to input their symptoms, and based on that information, it identifies potential diseases or health conditions. SWASTH goes beyond just diagnosis—it also offers a personalized daily lifestyle routine, tailored to help manage and improve the user’s health. By combining medical insights with practical wellness advice, SWASTH empowers individuals to take control of their well-being through informed, actionable steps.

Diseases

Malaria

A deadly disease caused by the Plasmodium parasite and transmitted by female Anopheles mosquitoes.

Tuberculosis

A potentially deadly disease caused by the Mycobacterium tuberculosis bacteria that attacks the lungs

COVID-19

An airborne disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus that can cause mild to moderate respiratory illness.

Diabetes

A chronic disease that occurs when the body doesn't produce enough insulin or can't use insulin properly.

Typhoid

A bacterial infection caused by the Salmonella Typhi bacteria that can be life-threatening if left untreated.

Cancer

A life-threatening disease in which some of the body's cells grow uncontrollably and spread to other parts of the body.
